# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `cordon` to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, respectful, and constructive.
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information, and any conduct that would be
inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or
reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban
contributors for serious or repeated violations.

To report an issue, open a confidential matter via GitHub or contact a
maintainer directly. Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith.
